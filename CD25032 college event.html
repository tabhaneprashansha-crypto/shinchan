About TechFest

Innovation Meets Creativity

TechFest 2026 is an annual technical event organized by the college to encourage students to showcase their technical skills, innovative ideas, and teamwork.

Participate in coding competitions, robotics, project exhibitions, workshops, and seminars conducted by industry experts.
